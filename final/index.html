Biography

XXXTentacion, born Jahseh Dwayne Ricardo Onfroy, was a controversial and influential artist known for his emotionally raw music. He rose to fame on SoundCloud and was tragically killed in 2018 at the age of 20.

Song Breakdown: Vice City

"Vice City" is one of XXXTentacion's early tracks, showcasing his lyrical prowess and dark themes. The song discusses the struggles of living a life surrounded by vice and temptation.
